Polyethylene terephthalate

PET

PET is also known as PETE or Polyester. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is commonly used for the packaging of Water and Beverages. PET is popular because it can be used to fill numerous chemicals and solutions. PET can also be very clear plastic and might seem like glass once set to its desired shape. PET can also be colour matched to the available range of colours in Preform.
